Dog Walking Services

What kind of walks does Cedar Dog offer?

If you want your furry friend to have company on their adventures, look no further than Cedar Dog! We are staffed with four professional dog walkers who absolutely love animals and will do everything they can to make sure your pets are taken care of on their walks. There are several packages available for both new and returning customers.

You can schedule a test walk for your pet to get to know our dog walkers. Or if you only need one appointment, we offer a One-Off walk for $19.99. Click here to schedule your appointment today!

Some of the dog walking packages we offer are:

  • Daily Package Deal
  • Weekly Package Deal
  • Furry Friends Package
  • Groups Galore Package

Want to meet the Cedar Dog dog walkers? Click here to learn more!

Daily Package Deal - $159.99

The Daily Package Deal covers daily walks for your pets! You can choose your own personal dogwalker and schedule a set time for your pets to get picked up as well as the length of the walk you want them to go on. If your pet is young and active and needs to get their energy out every day, then this is the perfect package for your pet.

This package covers daily walks for one pet, bundled Cedar Dog plan, your choice of walker, and standard walk size.

Weekly Package Deal - $49.99

If you have an elderly pet or only need to have your pet walked once a week, then this package has you covered. You can rest assured your pet will be safe and sound on their weekly walks and have a tone of fun!

This package covers walks once a week for one pet, basic Cedar Dog plan, available walkers, and small walk size.

Furry Friends Package - $299.99

Do you have multiple pets and not enough time on your hands? The Furry Friends Package might be for you! This package allows for you to get all of your pets on walks at once. You can choose to let them go on walks whenever for as long as an hour long walk. You can also schedule times to allow one of our walkers to take your pet on a hike. If you're wanting the standard Cedar Dog plan, then make sure to get this package today.

This package contains walks at any time, standard Cedar Dog plan, walker of your choice, any walk size, walks for up to three pets, and special deals.

Groups Galore Package - $699.99

If you want to pamper your pet, then look no further than the Groups Galore Package. This package contains the best services Cedar Dog has to offer. Cedar Dog occasionally caters events for pet owners. This package grants you and your pet special access to any deals, offers, and events we may be hosting. You can also gain special certificates for your pets that let everyone know how much they've been walking! If you want your furry friend to meet other dogs, then you can schedule a walk for groups of upwards to 12 dogs! Make sure to snag this package if you want the best for your pet.

This package contains Walkers Insurance, Dog Walker of your choice, group walks for your pets, pet playtime, hikes, walks of any size, premium Cedar Dog plan, pet certificate, and special deals and events.
