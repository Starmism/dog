Welcome to Cedar Dog!

Cedar Dog was founded 5 years ago in 2016 by Stephanie, Ethan, Hannah, and Maddox. Everyone in Cedar Dog loves animals and have been professional dog walkers for years. Over the years we have gathered over 30 clients in Cedar City. We offer safe and reliable services to keep your animals in shape and provide the opportunity for your dogs to meet other dogs!
